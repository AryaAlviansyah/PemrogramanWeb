About Gundam

Gundam (ガンダム Gandamu) is the name of a science fiction series created by studio Sunrise featuring a giant robot (mecha) named "Gundam" (same as the name of the first mecha). The series began on April 7, 1979 with Mobile Suit Gundam, a TV Series that defined the "true robot" anime genre by featuring giant robots called mobile suits in a military setting. The popularity of the series and its merchandise has resulted in a franchise that includes television series, OVAs, films, manga, novels and video games, as well as an industrial robot model known as Gunpla (plastic model Gundam).
